Frequently Asked Questions

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer flexible cancellation options. Most properties allow cancellation up to 48 hours before check-in for a full refund. Please check individual property policies for specific details.

Do you offer long-term discounts?

Yes! We offer special rates for stays of 30 days or more. Contact our reservations team for personalized quotes.

Are pets allowed?

Most of our properties are pet-friendly, but policies vary. Check the amenities section of each property or contact us for details.

Is parking included?

Parking availability and pricing vary by location. Refer to the property details or contact our team for specific information.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, bank transfers, and digital payment methods. Payment is secure and processed through our secure gateway.

How quickly will my inquiry be answered?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our 24/7 support line.

Nyodera Heights is a premier provider of luxury furnished apartments and Airbnb services. With over a decade of experience in the hospitality industry, we specialize in providing exceptional living spaces for both long-term residents and short-term visitors.
